Add New Items

The application includes a user-friendly form for adding new items to the library collection.

Add Form Features

  • Name Field: Title of the book/movie/magazine
  • Author Field: Author, director, or publisher
  • Date Selection: Calendar date picker
  • Category Dropdown: Book, Movie, or Magazine
  • Form Validation: Required field checking
  • Confirmation: Success feedback

The form includes client-side validation to ensure data integrity before submission.
